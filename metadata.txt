about=This plugin provides a suite of algorithms in the QGIS Processing toolbox for deriving Land Surface Temperature (LST) from thermal infrared satellite imagery. It supports Landsat 5 TM, Landsat 7 ETM+, Landsat 8 TIRS, and ASTER sensors.

    Workflow:
    1. Compute NDVI from red and near-infrared bands
    2. Convert thermal band digital numbers to spectral radiance
    3. Convert radiance to brightness temperature using sensor K1/K2 constants
    4. Estimate land surface emissivity using NDVI-based methods
    5. Retrieve land surface temperature

    Available algorithms:
    Vegetation Indices  : Landsat NDVI, ASTER NDVI
    Radiance            : TM Radiance, ETM+ Radiance, TIRS Radiance, ASTER Radiance
    Brightness Temp.    : Brightness Temperature (all sensors)
    Emissivity          : Zhang LSE, NDVI Threshold LSE, ASTER LSE
    LST                 : Planck Equation, Mono-Window Algorithm (Qin et al. 2001),
                          Single Channel Algorithm (Jimenez-Munoz & Sobrino 2003),
                          Radiative Transfer Equation, ASTER Single Channel,
                          ASTER Split-Window

    Temperature output available in Kelvin, Celsius, or Fahrenheit.
